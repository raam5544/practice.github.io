Beverage - Sweet Lassi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste) baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Crushed ice (optional)
  • Chopped pistachios or almonds for garnish/li>
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Cooking instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine yogurt, milk, sugar, and cardamom powder.
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. If desired, add crushed ice and blend again until well mixed.
  4. Pour the lassi into glasses and garnish with chopped pistachios or almonds.
  5. Top with mint leaves for a refreshing touch.
  6. Serve chilled and enjoy your Sweet Lassi.
