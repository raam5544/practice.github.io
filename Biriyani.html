Main Dish - Biriyani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1/4 fried onions (for garnish)
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves (for garnish)
  • Whole spices: bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon
  • Biriyani masala powder
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste

Cooking instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice and soak it for 30 minutes. Cook the rice until it's 70% done.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, biriyani masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. In a large pan, heat oil or shee. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  4. Add whole spices to the pan and saute until aromatic.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  6. Add marinated chicken and Cook until it's partially done.
  7. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken
  8. Sprinkle fried onions, coriander leaves, and mint leaves over the rice.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice and chicken are fully cooked.
  10. Fluff the biriyani gently and serve hot.
