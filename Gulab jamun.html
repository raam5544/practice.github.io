Dessert - Gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil or ghee for frying

Cooking instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, ghee, milk, and baking soda. Knead into a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round balls (jamuns).
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and fry the jamuns until they turn golden brown. Set aside.
  4. In a separate pan, make a sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water. Add cardamompowder and boil until it thickens slightly.
  5. Drop the fried jamuns into the sugar syrup and let them soak for a few hours or until they absorb the syrup.
  6. Serve the Gulab jamun warm, optionally garnished with chopped nuts.
