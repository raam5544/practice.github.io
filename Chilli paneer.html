Appetizer - Chilli paneer

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sause
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Cooking instructions:

  1. Coat paneer cubes with cornstarch and fry until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, saute garlic and green chilies
  3. Add sliced onions and bell peppers. Cook until slightly tender
  4. Combine soy sause, tomato ketchu, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Pour over the vegetable.
  5. Add fried paneer cubes and mix well.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix cornstarchwith waterto create a slurry. Pour into the pan to thicken the sauce.
  7. Garnish with spring onions and servehot.
