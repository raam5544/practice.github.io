Main Dish - Naan paneer

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or melted butter
  • 1/4 cup warm water (as needed for kneading)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt

Cooking instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix yogurt, sugar, milk, and ghee.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and knead into a soft dough. Add warm water if needed.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for 2 hours.
  5. Divide the dough into equal portion and roll each portion into a ball.
  6. Roll out each ball into a flat, oval shape (naan).
  7. Heat a skillet or tawa and cook each naan until it puffs up and has golden brown spots.
  8. Brushwith ghee or butter and serve hot.
